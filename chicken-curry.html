Simple Murgir Jhol (Chicken curry)

(Non-Vegetarian)

Its a delicious main course item. You can make this at your home with very simple ingredients. Everybody will love this recipe.




Ingredients


  • Chicken 10-15 pcs.
  • One big onion finely chopped.
  • One tomato chopped.
  • Ginger & garlic paste 2 tsp.
  • Whole spices (4 green cardemom, 3 cloves, 1 bay leaf, 1 small cinnamon stick, 5 whole black pepper).
  • Spice powder (turmeric 3 tsp, cumin 1 tsp, coriander 1 tsp, red chili 2 tsp).
  • Two green chilies.
  • Salt & sugar.
  • Mustard oil 6 tsp.
  • Warm water.

Steps


  • Marinate the chicken pieces with 2 tsp turmeric powder, 1 tsp red chili powder, 2 tsp mustard oil and some salt. Keep aside for 2 hours.
  • Take a pan. Heat the remaining mustard oil.
  • Add 2 tsp of sugar. Caramalize it.
  • Then add the whole spices. Saute it for a minute.
  • Add the chopped onion. Fry this till it gets brown.
  • Then add ginger & garlic paste. Mix it for 2 minutes.
  • Add the chopped tomatoes and some salt. Cook this for 10 minutes in slow flame.
  • Then add all the spice powder. Mix it and cook this another 7 minutes.
  • When the oil starts separating add the marinated chicken. Mix it very well.
  • First cook this chicken in high flame for 7 minutes. Then reduce the flame to medium and cook this with lid for 15 minutes.
  • You can see chicken will release its own juices.
  • Then add 2 cups of warm water and green chilies. Cook this for another 25 minutes.
  • Check the chicken. Gravy should be runny. Turn of your gas.

Your simple chicken curry is ready ...
  • Serve this hot with plain roti or rice ...
