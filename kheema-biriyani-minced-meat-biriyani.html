Kheema Biriyani (Minced meat Biriyani)

(Non-Vegetarian)

In India "biriyani" is a very popular dish. There are plenty of variations in biriyani. The process is a little lenthy but it tastes delicious. Yesterday I took an attempt on making Kheema biriyani, after the entire process I realize it is the easiest one. That is why I am sharing this recipe with you because it is one of the tastiest and easiest biriyani ever. Try this at your home and make yourself special.




Ingredients


  • 2 cups minced mutton.
  • Basmati rice 4 cups.
  • 1 onion sliced.
  • Ginger and garlic paste 2 tsp.
  • Curd 4 tsp.
  • Biriyani masala 5 tsp.( from any indian store).
  • Whole spices (cumin seed 1 tsp, 5 green cardemom, 6 cloves, 1 star anis, 1 small cinnamon stick).
  • Yellow food colour.
  • 2 green chilies.
  • Clarified butter/ghee 8 tsp.
  • 2 tsp rose water.
  • Salt and sugar.
  • Water.

Steps


  • Wash the basmati rice in cold water very well and soak this for 1 hour.
  • Take a pan. Heat 3 tsp ghee.
  • Add sliced onion in hot ghee and fry it till golden brown.
  • Then add ginger & garlic paste. Mix it for 3 minutes.
  • Then add the minced meat. Mix this very well.
  • Now add some salt, green chilies, biriyani masala and 4 tsp beaten curd. Mix this very well.
  • Add some warm water and cook this in slow flame for 30 minutes. Keep some gravy in the curry.
  • Meat should be 100% done and check the seasoning.
  • Now take another vessel. Boil lots of water.
  • Add 4 table spoon salt and whole spices in boiling water.
  • Then add the soaked rice.
  • When the rice will 80% done, drain all water.
  • Now take another pan. Put a layer of meat curry and then put a layer of rice. Put 2 tsp ghee and 1 tsp rose water on top of it.
  • Repeat the same process. Lastly add pinch of food color on top of the last layer of rice.
  • Cover the pan with a tight lid and turn on the gas.
  • Keep the gas on high flame first for 4 minutes, then bring it low for another 15 minutes.
  • Let it rest for 10 minutes then open the lid.

Your Kheema biriyani is ready ...
  • Enjoy this hot with raita or any type of gravy ...
