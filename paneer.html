Paneer (Indian Cottage Cheese)

(Vegetarian)

In vegetarian world paneer plays a very important role. But in nonvegrtarian world it also offers some special dishes. Homemade paneer has its own frshness and taste. So, lets see the recipe.




Ingredients


  • Full fat milk 2 lt.
  • One small bowl lemon juice.
  • Hunk beaten curd 5 tsp.

Steps


  • Take a vessel, pour the milk. Bring it to boil. Stir with a spatula occasionally.
  • When it starts boiling add the lemon juice and beaten curd.
  • Turn of the gas. Stir 1/2 times.
  • When the milk curdles strain the water.
  • Wash the curdled milk in cold water for 2-3 minutes. Again strain the water.

Your paneer is ready ...
  • Cook this paneer with any gravy or as appetizer ...
