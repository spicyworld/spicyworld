Paneer Makhani

(Vegetarian)

Its a main course dish. Paneer is also called Indian cottage cheese.




Ingredients


  • Paneer (Indian cottage cheese) 12-15 cubes
  • Two large tomato
  • One inch ginger
  • Two green chilies
  • Turmeric powder 1 tsp
  • Coriander powder 2 tsp
  • Cumin powder 2 tsp
  • Garam masala powder 1 tsp
  • Dry fenugreek leaves 2 tsp
  • Heavy whipped cream 1 small bowl
  • Salt and sugar as per your taste
  • Half cup of hot water
  • Butter 4 tsp

Steps


  • Make a paste of tomatoes, ginger and green chilies.
  • Shallow fry the paneer cubes in oil. Remove the paneer from pan.
  • After that add 3 tsp butter in hot pan. After melting the butter add no.5-no.8 ingredients. Saute it for 2 mins in slow flame.
  • Then add tomato paste. Stir well. Cook this with lid for 15 minutes in medium slow flame.
  • Then add salt and sugar as per your taste. Mix it.
  • Now add heavy cream in the gravy and stir very well.
  • After 3 mins add the hot water. Mix it.
  • Add the paneer cubes into the gravy. Cover the pan and cook it for 10 minutes in medium flame.
  • Then check the gravy. It should be medium thick like the picture.
  • Add some crushed dry fenugreek leaves. Mix it.
  • Finally add 1 tsp butter and turn off your gas.

Your Paneer Makhani is ready.
  • Serve hot with roti, naan, kulcha and pulao.
