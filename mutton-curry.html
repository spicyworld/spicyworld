Mutton er laal jhol (Mutton Curry)

(Non-Vegetarian)

This one is a Bengali mutton recipe. Almost every Sunday they make this curry as lunch or dinner. Its my pleasure to share this recipe with you. Please try this once.




Ingredients


  • Mutton 10-15 pieces.
  • One big onion finely chopped.
  • Ginger & garlic paste 2 tsp.
  • One tomato finely chopped.
  • Two green chilies.
  • Whole spices( 4 green cardemom, 4 cloves, 1 small cinnamon stick, 1 bay leaf, 5 whole black pepper )
  • Spice powder( 2 tsp turmeric, 1 tsp red chili, 1 tsp cumin, 1 tsp coriander )
  • Salt & sugar according to your taste.
  • 2 potatoes cut into halves.
  • Mustard oil 6 tsp.
  • Warm water.

Steps


  • Take a pressure cooker. Heat the mustard oil.
  • Add the potatoes. Fry this for 5 minutes. Keep aside.
  • Then add all the whole spices into remaining oil.
  • Add 2 tsp sugar and caramalize it.
  • Then add chopped onion. Fry it untill it become golden brown.
  • Then add ginger & garlic paste. Cook this for 2 minutes.
  • Add the chopped tomato and some salt. Mix it and cook this for 5 minutes in slow flame.
  • Then add all spice powder. Mix this very well and cook this another 3 minutes.
  • Now add the mutton pieces and green chilies. Mix this with the masala very well and cook this in slow flame for 12 minutes.
  • You will see mutton will release its own juice. You have to cook this mutton in its own juice for another 10 minutes.
  • Now add 2 cups of warm water. Stir this well and pressure this till 4 whistle. Let release the pressure on its own.
  • Then add the fried potatoes and a cup of warm water. Again pressure this for another 2 whistle.
  • After that check the seasoning and meat should become very tendor. The gravy should be little bit runny.

Your mutton er laal jhol is ready ...
  • Serve this hot with plain rice ...
