Chicken Lollipop

(Non-Vegetarian)

Its a spicy and delicious nonvegetarian starter item. This one is a Chinese style recipe. You will like it.




Ingredients


  • Chicken wing drummets 10 pieces.
  • Two eggs.
  • Cornflour 10 tsp.
  • Three inch ginger chopped.
  • Three cloves garlic chopped.
  • Two green chilies chopped.
  • White pepper powder 3 tsp.
  • Soy sauce 5 tsp.
  • Chili garlic sauce 5 tsp.
  • Tomato ketchup 2 tsp.
  • Msg 2 tsp.
  • Salt & sugar.
  • Chicken stock half cup.
  • One spring onion chopped.
  • White oil for deep fry.

Steps


  • To make the lollipop you have to prepare the wings by turning the meat portion inside out. Then you will have the lollipops.
  • Now take a bowl add eggs, cornflour, 1 tsp msg, 2 tsp white pepper powder, 3 tsp chili garlic sauce, 3 tsp soy sauce, some chopped ginger, green chilies and garlic, salt and sugar as per your taste. Mix it with your hand very well. Make a lump free batter.
  • Add the chicken into the batter and mix it again. Keep aside for 1-2 hours.
  • Take a vessel and heat lots of white oil for deep fry the chicken.
  • Add the chicken one by one into the hot oil and fry it very well. Dont overcrowd the vessel. Make the chicken very crispy. Keep aside.
  • Now take a wok. Heat 3 tsp oil in high flame.
  • Add rest of the chopped ginger, garlic and green chilies. Saute it for a minute.
  • Then add the rest of chili garlic sauce, soy sauce, msg and white pepper powder. Mix it very well.
  • Add tomato ketchup, salt, sugar and some chicken stock. Cook it for 4 minutes in high flame.
  • Now add the fried chicken into the sauce. Toss this very well.
  • Finally sprinkle some spring onion and turn off your gas.
  • Before adding the chicken the sauce should be thick not runny.
  • Dont cook the chicken pieces with the sauce for long time. Otherwise it will become soggy. It should be crispy.

Your chicken lollipop is ready ...
  • Serve this hot with some salad and enjoy ...!!
