Macher Kalia (Different Style)

(Non-Vegetarian)

We Bengalis are very fond of fish. Our common meal is fish curry and rice. This recipe is one of the fish curry which we make in occasions and gatherings. I already gave you one kaliya recipe and this one is also very tasty. Try this at your home and let me know how it turns out.




Ingredients


  • 4 with bone fish pieces (rui / rohu / katla).
  • 4 tsp onion paste.
  • 1 tsp ginger paste.
  • 3 tsp hung curd.
  • 3 tsp turmeric powder.
  • 1 tsp cumin powder.
  • 1 tsp coriander powder.
  • Half tsp red chili powder.
  • Whole spices ( 1 small cinnamon stick, 3 green cardemoms, 3 cloves, 1 bay leaf)
  • Salt & sugar.
  • 2 green chilies.
  • 8 tsp mustard oil.
  • Warm water.
  • 1 tsp ghee / clarified butter.

Steps


  • Wash the fish pieces very well and marinate those with some salt and 2 tsp of turmeric powder. Keep aside for 20 minutes.
  • Take a pan. Heat 8 tsp mustard oil.
  • Add the marinated fish pieces in hot oil and fry both sides untill golden brown. Then remove those from oil.
  • In the same oil add whole spices. Saute it for 2 minutes.
  • Add onion paste. Fry it for 5 minutes.
  • Now take a small bowl. Add hung curd, little water, cumin and coriander powder, ginger paste, turmeric & red chili powder. Mix it very well and add into the pan. Cook this for another 4 minutes.
  • When oil starts separate add fried fish pieces. Mix this very well.
  • Then add some warm water, green chilies, salt and half tsp sugar. Cook this for 5 minutes.
  • Check the seasoning.
  • Turn off your gas and add 1 tsp ghee.

Your Fish Kalia is ready ...
  • Enjoy this with hot rice ...
