Chicken Tikka Kebab

(Non-Vegetarian)

Its an awesome starter dish.




Ingredients


  • Boneless chicken cut into cubes (40 pieces)
  • 1 onion cut into cubes
  • 1 green and red bell pepper cut into cubes
  • Cumin seeds 3 tsp
  • Coriander seeds 4 tsp
  • Whole black pepper 1 tsp
  • 2 dry red chili
  • 4 green cardamom
  • 1 small cinnamon stick
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Fennel seeds 1 tsp
  • Hunk curd 6 tsp
  • Red food color a pinch
  • Salt and sugar as per your taste
  • Mustard / white oil 4 tsp
  • Dry fenugreek leaves 2 tsp
  • Bamboo skewers 10 pieces

Steps


  • Take a microwave safe bowl. Put no.4-no.11 in that bowl. Heat those in microwave for 2-3 minutes.
  • Put those in a grinder and make a powder. This is tikka masala.
  • For the marination take a bowl. Put the chicken pieces.
  • Add hunk curd.
  • Add tikka masala.
  • Add salt and sugar as per your taste.
  • Add red food color.
  • Add crushed dry fenugreek leaves.
  • Add oil and mix this very well.
  • Put the marination into the refrigerator for at least 4 hrs.
  • For best result keep it overnight.
  • Soak the bamboo skewers into water for 30 minutes before putting in the oven.
  • When the marination is done take a skewer , put a onion cube then a chicken piece then a bell pepper cube. Repeat this process with all the skewers. (take a look of the picture)
  • Preheat the oven in 400o F for 15 minutes.
  • Brush some oil on the chicken and put the skewers into the oven.
  • Put the chickens closer to the filaments.
  • Turn on the oven in 400o F for 25 minutes.
  • After that take out those, turn the other side and brush some oil.
  • Again put those into the oven in 450o F for 15 minutes.
  • After that take out your kebabs from oven.

Your Chicken Tikka Kebab is ready.
  • Sprinkle some lime juice / chaat masala over your kebabs and enjoy ..!!
  • Every oven has its own temperature. Adjust the temperature according to your oven. I am sharing this according to my oven. So for the safety check the kebabs 1/2 times during the grilling process.
