Mutton Stew

(Non-Vegetarian)

During summer we generally avoid to eat rich food. But when it comes to mutton, the decision become difficult ðŸ˜‰. For this kind of situation 'mutton stew' is the perfect food for all. The recipe is very simple and have some mild flavours. You will love this when you make it.




Ingredients


  • 10 pieces of mutton.
  • 6 tsp of hung curd.
  • 1 onion thinly sliced.
  • 1 tsp of ginger and garlic paste.
  • 2 green chilies.
  • 3 tsp of white oil.
  • 3 green cardamoms.
  • 2 bay leaves.
  • 2 tsp turmeric powder.
  • Salt and sugar.
  • Warm water.

Steps


  • Marinate the mutton pieces with hung curd, ginger and garlic paste, 1 tsp turmeric powder and some salt for 2-3 hours.
  • Then heat oil in a pressure cooker.
  • Add the bay leaves and green cardamoms. Saute it for a minute.
  • Then add the onion slices. Fry this till golden.
  • Add the marinated mutton. Mix it well for 6 minutes.
  • Add some salt, pinch of sugar, green chilies and remaining turmeric powder. Mix it for another 6 minutes.
  • Add 2 cups of warm water and cover the cooker. Wait for 6 whistles or untill the meat is done.

Your Mutton stew is ready ...
  • Enjoy this with toasted bread or white rice ...
