Basonti Pulaao

(Vegetarian)

Its a traditional bengali dish. "Basanti" means yellow colour. They make this in many gatherings. You can also call this as sweet pulao.




Ingredients


  • Kalijira (gobindovog) rice 2 cups.
  • Finely chopped some (carrot, capsicum, french beans).
  • Some green peas.
  • 8 cashews.
  • 8 raisins.
  • Two green chilies.
  • Clarified butter / ghee 7 tsp.
  • Green cardemom 5.
  • One small cinnamon stick.
  • Four cloves.
  • One bay leaf.
  • Yellow food color.
  • Salt & sugar.
  • Milk 6 tsp.
  • Warm water.

Steps


  • Soak the rice in cold water for 30 minutes.
  • Take a pan. Heat ghee.
  • Now add green cardemom cloves, bay leaf, cinnamon stick. Saute it for a minute.
  • Add all the vegetables, cashews and raisins. Fry it for 3 minutes.
  • Then add the soaked rice. Saute it for 2 minutes.
  • Now add yellow food color, 5 cups of warm water, 3 tsp of salt and 6 tsp of sugar. (Adjust this as per your taste) Mix it very well.
  • Add the green chilies and milk. Again mix it.
  • Cover the pan with a tight lid. Keep it in slow flame for 25 minutes.
  • Check the rice. Switch of the gas and keep it another 20 minutes with the lid on.

Your basanti pulao is ready ...
  • Serve hot with any type of gravy.
