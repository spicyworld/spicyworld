Fish Mustard Curry

(Non-Vegetarian)

Its a main course dish.




Ingredients


  • Fish (in foreign countries you can take Pompano / Tilapia and in India you can take any fishes) 4 pieces.
  • Black mustard seeds 2 tsp.
  • Yellow mustard seeds 3 tsp.
  • Three green chilies.
  • Nigella seeds half tsp.
  • Dry mango powder half tsp.
  • Turmeric powder.
  • Half cup of hot water.
  • One potatoes cut into wedges.
  • Mustard oil 1 small bowl.
  • Salt and sugar as per your taste.

Steps


  • Soak the black and yellow mustard seeds for 1-2 hrs. Then put those in a grinder along with two green chilies, 3 pinches of salt and some water. Make a thick paste.
  • Wash the fish pieces thoroughly into the water.
  • Then marinate the pieces with 1 tsp salt and 1 tsp turmeric powder for 30 minutes.
  • Heat mustard oil in a pan. Fry the fish pieces from both side in the oil.
  • Then remove it from oil.
  • Now fry the potatoes 50%.
  • Then remove the potatoes.
  • As we make the gravy with the same oil so we need only 3 tsp oil. Then remove the excess oil.
  • Add nigella seeds and green chilies into the hot oil.
  • Then add 4 tsp mustard paste and 1 tsp turmeric powder. Saute it for a minute.
  • Add the hot water and salt. Mix it well for 3 minutes.
  • Add fish pieces and potatoes. Cover the pan and cook it for 15 minutes in slow flame.
  • After that check the gravy and potatoes. it should be cooked 100%.
  • Finally add dry mango powder. Mix it and turn off your gas.

Your Fish Mustard Curry is ready.
  • Serve hot with plain rice.
