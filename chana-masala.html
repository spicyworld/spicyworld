Chana masala

(Vegetarian)

Its a main course dish. Also called Curry of Garbanzo Beans




Ingredients


  • Chana (garbanzo beans) 1 small bowl.
  • One big finely chopped onion.
  • Ginger and garlic paste 1 tsp.
  • One big fresh tomato.
  • Two green chilies.
  • Cumin seeds 1 tsp.
  • Two dry red chili.
  • Two bay leaves.
  • Turmeric powder 1 tsp.
  • Cumin powder 1 tsp.
  • Coriander powder 1 tsp.
  • Chana masala powder 2 tsp. ( u can get from Indian store )
  • Dry fenugreek leaves 3 tsp
  • Chopped coriander leaves half small bowl.
  • Salt and sugar as per your taste.
  • Mustard oil / White oil 5 tsp.
  • Hot water 1 cup.
  • One inch ginger , cut into julian pieces.
  • Half onion cut into rings.

Steps


  • Soak the garbanzo beans / chana in water overnight.
  • Then boil the beans/chana 60%.
  • Make a paste of tomatoes and green chilies. Keep aside.
  • Heat oil in a pan.
  • Add no.6,7,8 into the hot oil. Saute it for a minute.
  • Then add chopped onion. Fry this until it become golden brown.
  • Add ginger and garlic paste. Cook it for 3 minutes.
  • Then add tomato paste. Stir well.
  • Cover the pan and cook it for 12 minutes in medium flame.
  • After that add no. 9,10,11,12. Mix it for 5 minutes.
  • Add salt and sugar. Mix it.
  • Now add the beans/chana. Stir well.
  • Add the hot water. Cover the pan and cook it for 20 mins in medium slow flame.
  • Check the gravy. It should be medium thin.Also check the beans. It should be 100% cooked.
  • Then add crushed fenugreek leaves and chopped coriander leaves. Mix it well.
  • Finally add onion rings and ginger julians. Then turn off your gas.

Your Chana masala is ready.
  • Serve hot with naan, paratha, kulcha.
