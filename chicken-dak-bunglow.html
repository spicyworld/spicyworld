Chicken dak bunglow

(Non-Vegetarian)

The name of this recipe is very funny. But it tastes delicious when you make this with your love and care. Lets give it a try.




Ingredients


  • Chicken 10-15 pieces.
  • Four baby potatoes.
  • Four hard boil eggs.
  • One onion thinly sliced.
  • Ginger & garlic paste 2 tsp.
  • One tomato chopped.
  • Hunk curd 4 tsp.
  • Two green chilies.
  • Whole spices( 4 green cardemom, 4 cloves, 1 small cinnamon stick, 1 bay leaf, 5 whole black pepper )
  • Spice powder( 2 tsp turmeric, 1 tsp red chili, 1 tsp cumin, 1 tsp coriander )
  • Salt & sugar.
  • Mustard oil.
  • Warm water.

Steps


  • Marinate the chicken pieces with hunk curd, spice powder. Keep this aside for 1 hour.
  • Shallow fry the potatoes for 5 minutes and eggs for 2 minutes. Keep aside.
  • Make a paste of whole spices with 4 tsp of water. Keep aside.
  • Take a pan and heat the mustard oil.
  • Add chopped onion in hot. Fry this for 10 minutes.
  • Add ginger & garlic paste. Cook this for 2 minutes.
  • Add chopped tomato, salt and some sugar. Cook this in slow flame for 6 minutes.
  • Now add the marinated chicken. Mix it very well in high flame for 7 minutes.
  • Add the fried potatoes , green chilies and some warm water. Cook this in slow flame for 30 minutes.
  • After that add the boiled eggs and the whole spice paste. Cook this another 2 minutes.
  • Check the seasoning, chicken and potatoes should be tendor and gravy should be thick.

Your chicken dak bunglow is ready ...
  • Serve this hot with plain rice or roti ...
