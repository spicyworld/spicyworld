Chicken Korma

(Non-Vegetarian)

Its an awesome non vegetarian main course dish. In India its a very popular dish. We make this in many occasions. Hope you will like it.




Ingredients


  • Chicken legs 4 pieces.
  • Brown onion 1 cup.
  • Ginger & garlic paste 2 tsp.
  • Two green chilies.
  • Salt & sugar.
  • Hunk curd 1 cup.
  • One cinnamon stick.
  • Five green cardemoms.
  • Four cloves.
  • One star anis.
  • Turmeric powder 2 tsp.
  • Coriander powder 1 tsp.
  • Cumin powder 1 tsp.
  • Nutmeg powder half tsp.
  • Mace powder half tsp.
  • Red chili powder 2 tsp.
  • Clarified butter/ ghee 6 tsp.
  • Mustard oil 2 tsp.
  • Kewra water half tsp.
  • Warm water.

Steps


  • Marinate the chicken pieces with 1 tsp turmeric,red chilli powder,mustadr oil and salt. Keep aside for 1 hour.
  • Make a paste of brown onion , half cup curd and two green chilies. Keep aside.
  • Now add all the powder and 5 tsp water in the remaining curd. Stir it very well. Keep aside.
  • Take a pan. Heat 2 tsp ghee and 1 tsp mustard oil.
  • Add the marinated chicken legs and fry in high flame for 3 minutes. Then keep aside.
  • Now in that pan add the remaining ghee. Heat it.
  • Add cinnamon stick,cloves,green cardemoms and star anis. Saute it for a minute.
  • Then add the ginger & garlic paste. Fry it for 2 minutes.
  • Add the curd & powder mixture. Cook this on slow flame for 20 minutes.
  • Then add brown onion mixture,salt and some sugar. Stir this very well and cook it another 10 minutes.
  • Now add the fried chicken legs and kewra water. Mix it well and add some warm water. Cook this in slow flame for 25 minutes.
  • Check the gravy and the chicken. Turn off your gas.

Your chicken korma is ready .... Enjoy ...!!
  • Serve hot with biriyani, naan, pulao.
