Kadai Chicken

(Non-Vegetarian)

Its a main course dish.




Ingredients


  • Cumin seeds 3 tsp
  • Coriander seeds 3 tsp
  • Fennel seeds 3 tsp
  • Dry red chilies 2
  • Whole black pepper 1 tsp
  • Dry fenugreek leaves 3 tsp
  • One medium onion chopped
  • One small onion , cut into cube shape
  • Ginger and garlic paste 2 tsp
  • Two tomato puree
  • Turmeric powder 1 tsp
  • Chicken 12 pieces
  • Salt and sugar as per your taste
  • White oil 5 tsp
  • Chopped coriander leaves 3 tsp
  • One medium green and red bell pepper , cut into cube shape
  • Two green chilies

Steps


  • Take a microwave proof bowl. Put 1-6 ingredients. Heat it in microwave for 2 mins. Then grind it in a grinder. This is kadai masala.
  • Take a pan. Turn the gas on. Heat oil.
  • Add chopped onion. Saute it for 5-7 minutes. Then add turmeric powder, salt and sugar.
  • Add ginger and garlic paste. Cook this till the raw flavour is gone.
  • Add the chicken pieces. Mix it. Cook this with a lid on for 15 minutes in slow flame.
  • After that add 2 tsp of Kadai masala. Mix it.
  • Add tomato puree and green chilis. Mix it well. Cook this for 10-15 minutes in medium flame.
  • After that add cube size onion and bell peppers. Mix it very well. Cook this for only 2 minutes.
  • Finally add some left over Kadai masala and chopped coriander leaves. Mix it one more time. Then turn off your gas.

Your kadai chicken is ready.
  • Serve hot with roti, naan, rice or kulcha.
