Mutton Pulao / Yakhni Pulao

(Non-Vegetarian)

This recipe is an authentic pulao recipe. In any gathering you can make this dish and it will be blast. This pulao is super tasty and very easy to make. You just have to be perfect about the measurement. Please try this at your home.




Ingredients


  • 10 small size mutton pieces.
  • 2 cups basmati rice.
  • 1 small onion cut into 4 pieces and 1 medium size onion thinly sliced.
  • 2 inch ginger.
  • 4 cloves garlic.
  • Whole spices (5 green cardamoms, 6 cloves, 2 small cinnamon stick, 10 whole black pepper, 4 dry red chili).
  • 2 tsp roasted coriander powder.
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds.
  • 1 tsp ghee.
  • 4 tsp white oil.
  • Salt.
  • Warm water.

Steps


  • Wash the rice very well and soak in cold water for 1 hour.
  • Take a pressure cooker. Put the mutton pieces, big 4 pieces of onion, ginger, garlic, cumin seeds, coriander powder, whole spices, 3 tsp salt and 2 cups of water. Mix these things with a spatula and pressure cook this untill the meat become tender. Keep aside.
  • The juice of the meat should be salty because the rice will cook in this juice.
  • Now take a pan. Heat 4 tsp white oil.
  • Add thinly sliced onion. Fry it till golden. Do not make the onion dark.
  • In this add boiled mutton along with all those spices, onion, ginger and garlic. Keep the juice separately.
  • Cook this entire thing for 10 minutes in high flame. You have to give the meat a nice brown color.
  • After that put all the juices into the meat and also some warm water. Let it boil.
  • Taste the juice at this point. It should be salty.
  • Now add the soaked rice and 1 tsp ghee into the boiling juice. Stir this once, cover the pan and cook it untill the water dry out.
  • Here measurement is very important. The ratio of the juice and rice should be perfect. Because you will cook the rice with the juice and meat.

Your Mutton pulao is ready ...
  • Enjoy this hot with any type of gravy ...
